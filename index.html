VIP packaging concept

Jetour G700 VIP Box

A premium presentation box designed to hold the Jetour G700 keys, NFC card, certificate, and calling card in a refined handover experience.

Client
Jetour
Role
3D design, packaging concept, rendering
Items
G700 keys, NFC card, certificate, calling card
Output
VIP box concept and presentation renders

Background

The task was to design a VIP box that could organize every ownership item into one precise, ceremonial package.

Concept

The layout uses custom-cut compartments, black leather textures, chrome hardware, and layered card placements to make the handover feel structured and premium.

Highlight

Every object has a deliberate place.

The tray separates the keys, NFC card, certificate, and calling card while keeping the reveal visually balanced from the moment the case opens.

Commemorative coin set

Qatar Steel Coin Set

A VIP presentation set designed around four collectible coins, each representing one of Qatar Steel's core product lines.

Client
Qatar Steel
Role
3D design, product concept, packaging render
Items
Four product coins and VIP packaging set
Output
Coin set concept and presentation renders

Background

The task was to create a premium coin set for Qatar Steel that could communicate the company's products through a collectible VIP gift.

Concept

Each coin uses sculpted product imagery to represent a different offering: cold direct reduced iron, hot briquetted iron, reinforcing steel bars, and steel billets.

Highlight

Four products, one ceremonial set.

The coin arrangement turns Qatar Steel's product range into a compact object system, supported by a black-and-burgundy packaging language.

Kids first aid kit

Oryx GTL First Aid Pouch

A child-friendly first aid pouch concept designed for Oryx GTL, with attachable pins, a soft red pouch system, and waterproof sticker sheets.

Client
Oryx GTL
Role
Pouch design, pin design, sticker design, rendering
Items
Kids first aid pouch, pins, waterproof stickers
Output
Product concept and presentation renders

Background

Oryx GTL wanted a first aid pouch for kids that could feel approachable, useful, and personal through pins children can attach to the pouch.

Concept

The design uses a bright red pouch, visible zipper details, mesh storage, allergy and safety pins, and waterproof stickers that extend the same visual system.

Highlight

A practical pouch made more personal for kids.

The attachable pins help children mark allergies, medical alerts, and safety needs, while the pouch keeps the system portable and easy to recognize.
